About

I'm Aniket Pathak, a software engineer focused on healthcare integrations. Most of my work lives in the space between vendor EHRs — eClinicalWorks, Epic, Athena, NextGen — and the product teams trying to build on top of them.

My day-to-day: FHIR transforms, REST APIs, token dances, edge cases nobody documented, and the kind of quiet plumbing that either works every time or gets paged about at 2 a.m.
